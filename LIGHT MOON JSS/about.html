Our Story

Lightmoon Junior Secondary School was founded in 1995 with a vision to provide quality education to the community. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most respected institutions in the region.

1995 - School Founded

Established with just 50 students and 5 teachers in a small building.

2002 - First Expansion

Added science laboratories and a library to enhance learning.

2010 - Sports Complex

Built our sports facilities including football field and basketball courts.

2020 - Digital Learning

Implemented computer labs and digital learning platforms.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops students intellectually, physically, socially and morally, preparing them to be responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures well-rounded individuals capable of critical thinking, innovation and positive contribution to society.
