Our Academic Program

At Lightmoon JSS, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet national standards while challenging students to reach their full potential.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is based on the national education framework but enhanced with our own innovative teaching methods and enrichment programs. We focus on:

  • Core academic subjects
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Technology integration
  • Character development
  • Creative and performing arts
