Join Our School Community

We welcome applications from students who demonstrate academic potential, good character, and a willingness to contribute positively to our school community.

Admission Process

1

Application

Submit a completed application form along with required documents.

2

Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance assessment.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and parents attend an interview.

4

Offer

Successful applicants receive an admission offer letter.

Requirements

For New Students (Grade 7)

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Previous school report cards (last 2 years)
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
  • Passport-sized photographs (4 copies)

For Transfer Students

  • All requirements for new students
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Current year's report card

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid to qualified students. Applications are considered on a case-by-case basis.

Contact our admissions office for more information about scholarship opportunities.
