Our World-Class Facilities

Lightmoon JSS boasts modern facilities designed to support all aspects of student development - academic, physical, social and creative.

Modern Classrooms

Our spacious, well-ventilated classrooms are equipped with:

  • Interactive whiteboards
  • Ergonomic furniture
  • Digital projectors
  • Optimal lighting for learning
  • Climate control systems

Class sizes are kept small (maximum 25 students) to ensure individual attention.

Resource Center & Library

Our state-of-the-art library provides:

  • Over 15,000 print resources
  • Digital library with e-books and online databases
  • Quiet study areas
  • Group discussion rooms
  • Computer stations for research

Professional librarians are available to assist students with research and reading recommendations.
